

On a snowy night with no moon, the cold wind howled, whimpering like a mountain ghost in the dark mountains.

There was a big hole in the roof of the dilapidated house in the mountain village, and the snowflakes fell little by little. The bright pattern lamps on the wall accompanied by a crackling bonfire illuminated the dilapidated house.

Xu Yuan was sitting on a blanket, wearing a dark brown mink fur, and was fiddling with a half-foot-high black porcelain pot in his hand.

Inside the porcelain jar was his supper with Ran Qingmo tonight.
