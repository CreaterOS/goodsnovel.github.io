

The torrential river, the gurgling water.

It seems to flow forever, without beginning or end.

The water systems, rivers and lakes in Lanzhou are quite complex and numerous, intertwined and connected with each other, forming an excellent water transportation.

Especially the area around Jiayuan City, the largest city in Lanzhou.

The prosperity of its water transportation is the best in Lanzhou. All kinds of wooden boats, large and small, are constantly running in the Xianglu Grand Canal and other natural rivers and lakes day and night.
