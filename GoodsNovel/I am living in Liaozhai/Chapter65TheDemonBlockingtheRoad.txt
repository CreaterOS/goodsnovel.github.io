Yuan Yutang didn't relax his vigilance, he listened to the six directions, watched the front and back, carefully squinted his eyes and walked forward.

After nearly two sticks of incense, the misty light of the crystal wall channel weakened in vain, and a large area of ​​darkness loomed ahead.

Yuan Yutang's heart shuddered, and he tightened the wooden stick in his hand for self-defense...
