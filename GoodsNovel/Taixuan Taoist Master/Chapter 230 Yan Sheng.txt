

Shen Yu walked out of the gate, and then went straight to a quiet house planted with peach trees on the opposite corner of the street, with the word Yao Chi engraved on it.

This street is less than a thousand feet in total, a horizontal line, with the houses of the Nine Holy Lands on both sides. According to the jokes of the children in the city, the monks of Lingyin Temple are chanting scriptures on the east side, and the warriors on the west side are yelling at bald donkeys to death.

And the distance between Daozong's house and Yaochi's house is only about three hundred feet.

The door was closed tightly, and Shen Yu was silent for a while, smelling the faint fragrance of flowers and the unique body fragrance of women in the air, and then knocked on the door slowly.

It had been more than half a month since he came to Wanliu City, and only then did he remember that Ye Xingchen had given him a letter...
