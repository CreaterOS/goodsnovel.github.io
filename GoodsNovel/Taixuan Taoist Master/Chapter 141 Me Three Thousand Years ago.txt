

Chapter 153 Omniscience

The man in the yellow robe was stunned. He turned his head and saw an old dog with white and white spots appearing behind him. It didn't move. It seemed to be queuing up.

It is a great humiliation for a dignified prince of the Tang Dynasty to be accused of blocking a dog's way in public.

The young man's expression finally became indifferent, and he said, "How about not giving way, a mere dog deserves me to make way for it?"

Shen Yu thought for a while, and said seriously: "You should really give way to it."

Li An, the third prince of the Tang Dynasty, had a sarcasm on his face. He was about to say something, but suddenly his expression changed, as if he remembered...
