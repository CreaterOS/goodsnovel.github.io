

Almost at the same time, the streets of Wanliu City shook downwards and collapsed by a foot.

The already dilapidated streets on both sides have completely become ruins at this moment.

Countless dust rolled out and spread out to both sides, blocking the sight of countless people.

Shen Yu fell from the sky and slowly walked into the smoke and dust.

At this time, countless figures rushed in at a faster speed, and then stopped in front of Shen Yu.

Behind the crowd, Bian Yong lay limp and half-lying by the wall, his pale cheeks were slightly rosy, and blood flowed from the corner of his mouth. There were faint bloodstains on his body, but it was not strong.

Just like the third son of Qin before, the sword energy brushed from all around, leaving countless wounds, none of which were fatal.
