

When Shen Yu came out of the cave, the sun was setting, the sky was covered with burning clouds, and the breeze was blowing from the distant mountains and forests, like a picture scroll.

Shen Yu took a breath of fresh mountain air, and the fatigue in her body disappeared instantly.

Not far away on a wooden chair in the open space, the old man sat there and asked, "Aren't you going to study any more?"

Shen Yu shook her head and said, "Break meeting, it took me a while to watch this time."

Wen Ruohai nodded and said, "There are chairs next to you, sit on whatever you want."

Shen Yu nodded, sat on a chair, and then looked at the sky with closed eyes.

The light at curtain time is as beautiful as illusion, and the sky is as beautiful as colorful silk and satin, and the sound of the forest whistling along with the wind...
