

Sanwazi is very happy these days.

There was no more yin and yang noise in Xiang'er Street. The old master didn't know whether he was scared of being beaten or what, and he never came here again.

The long-lost tranquility was restored on the street, and the days passed slowly like this.

Sanwazi's chopping of firewood became more and more stable, his cooking became better and better, and he read more and more books. His expression was calm, and he no longer looked like a child.

Finally, one afternoon, Shen Yu said, "It's fine."

Sanwazi put down the book and blinked.

Shen Yu said: "Soon there will be a child test, you go to participate."

Sanwazi was startled when he heard this, although every three years there is a children's test, and only after that...
