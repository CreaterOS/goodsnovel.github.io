

Shen Yu opened her eyes again and found that she had reached the top of a city wall.

Looking around, the ground and the city walls were filled with bright red blood everywhere.

The ground is full of ruins, corpses are everywhere, and the smoke of gunpowder is still floating in the distance.

It turned out to be the site of an ancient battlefield.

There are too many evil and hostile auras around, and they all turned into thick fog, floating in the air.

In the distant sky is a slowly flowing black mist, which cannot be seen clearly, but it is crying out in pain.

Seeing this scene, Shen Yu raised her head slightly with memory in her eyes.

There was also a woman standing on the opposite city wall.
