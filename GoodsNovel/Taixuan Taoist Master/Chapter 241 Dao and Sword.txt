Many chairs were placed under the willow trees in the rather desolate courtyard. The northern border was desolate. Although everyone present were practitioners, Ling Jianzi still lit a bonfire in the center, which seemed to be more in line with the current atmosphere.

The young man was dressed in coarse linen clothes, with clear eyes, and a rusty spear on his shoulders, facing the eyes of everyone, he was still a little bit shy.

Ling Jianzi raised his eyebrows slightly, looking very serious, and looked at the other party from time to time.
