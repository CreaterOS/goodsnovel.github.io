On the square of Tiankui Peak, disciples from all peaks and valleys gathered, shoulder to shoulder, chatting together.

Nearly a thousand disciples gathered in the huge square, except for those inner disciples and direct disciples who were dressed in auras, most of them were blue-clothed outer disciples and gray-clothed miscellaneous disciples.
