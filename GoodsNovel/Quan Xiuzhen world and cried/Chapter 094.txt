

Flocks of mayflies cover the sky and block out the sun, coming menacingly.

Jiang Yuebai dashed left and right in the fog, trying his best to dodge the swarm of mayflies.

This formation is a water formation, with the characteristics of water holding everything, any attack will be swallowed up, turned into the power of a swarm of mayflies, and finally counterattacks itself.

It's just that if you don't resist, you will be exhausted a little bit of spiritual energy. These mayflies are not real flying insects. They are cold with water, and if they penetrate into the body, they will only hinder the movement of spiritual energy.
