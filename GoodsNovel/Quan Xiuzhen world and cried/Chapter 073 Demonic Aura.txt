

The moon is dark and the wind is high, and the rain is torrential.

Lu Ying squatted under the courtyard wall, biting her hands, her whole body was drenched, her face was shivering, not knowing whether it was rain or tears.

Squeak—ah——

The sound of the door opening echoed in the rain, piercing to the bone.

Lu Ying suddenly stood up and turned around, staggered to support the wall, and saw Jiang Yuebai's face was pale, standing under the eaves, holding a grass doll in her hand.

Lu Ying backed away in fear, her mouth slightly parted and her jaw trembling, and it took a long time for her to make a sound from her throat.
