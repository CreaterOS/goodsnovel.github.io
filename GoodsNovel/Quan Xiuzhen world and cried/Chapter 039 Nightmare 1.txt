

"Jiang Yuebai, the handyman, there is an order from the Internal Affairs Hall. Starting today, you will be transferred to serve Elder Xiangtian in Hedan Hall Lin. Leave quickly, the order is irresistible!"

Outside the small green brick courtyard of Huaxi Valley, Jiang Yuebai stared numbly at the seal of the Law Enforcement Hall on the courtyard gate, and a flying sword from the Internal Affairs Hall flew over, leaving an order.

As the sky was approaching, Jiang Yuebai's eyes were red and swollen, and she looked down at the two things in her hands.

The left hand is her handyman token.

The right hand is a piece of wood given by Mo Baichun.
